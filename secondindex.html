Importance of Water

Water is essential for life. It is crucial for drinking, sanitation, agriculture, and industry. A reliable supply of clean water is vital for health and well-being.

Current Situation of Water Scarcity

India faces a significant water crisis, with over 600 million people experiencing extreme water stress. Groundwater levels are declining, and many regions suffer from drought.

Causes of Water Scarcity

  • Rapid population growth
  • Over-extraction of groundwater
  • Pollution of water bodies
  • Climate change and erratic rainfall
  • Inefficient water management

Possible Solutions

Addressing water scarcity requires a multifaceted approach:

  • Rainwater harvesting
  • Wastewater treatment and recycling
  • Public awareness campaigns
  • Improving irrigation practices
  • Policy reforms and better water management
